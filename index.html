Cost Of Conquest
What is Cost Of Conquest

This is a 3D turn-based strategy game where the player must defeat the AI by moving units and attacking their bases. The game will feature AI-controlled NPCs, randomly and procedurally generated maps, and 3D animated characters. In this epic game, the player will need to constantly upgrade their bases, manage their units, and devise cunning strategies to emerge victorious against the AI. The game will utilize a combination of C#, artificial intelligence, 3D animation, the Unity engine, pathfinding, and finite state machines.
